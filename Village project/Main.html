Bharat is a vibrant village situated in Arwal,Bihar, known for its strong community values, natural beauty, and agricultural roots. Despite its potential, the village has faced challenges in infrastructure, education, healthcare, and livelihood.

This platform shares updates on development efforts, government schemes, success stories, and opportunities for collaboration. Join us in making Bharat a beacon of rural progress.

Development Projects

Road Construction

New concrete road built to improve connectivity across the village, completed under PMGSY.

School Renovation

Village school upgraded with new classrooms, benches, and a smart learning system.

Solar Street Lights

Installation of solar-powered street lights to ensure safety and sustainability.

Health Camp

Free health checkups, vaccinations, and maternal health awareness conducted monthly.

Drinking Water Project

Construction of a water tank and pipelines to ensure safe drinking water for all households.

Skill Training Program

Youth trained in tailoring, mobile repair, and digital literacy to boost employment.
